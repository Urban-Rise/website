Urban-Rise Website
Official digital platform of MABA / Urban-Rise Alliance.
Integrating artificial intelligence, sustainable manufacturing, and community empowerment.

Overview
This repository hosts the dynamic website of the MABA initiative.
It reflects our vision of transforming ideas into living production spaces that respect environment, culture, and community.
The site presents projects, provides technical and educational documentation, and enables engagement with local and global audiences.

Objectives
Present ongoing and future projects of MABA and Urban-Rise Alliance.

Highlight smart kiosks, urban applications, and community-driven solutions.

Provide resources for developers, educators, and community members.

Support symbolic campaigns and interactive storytelling.

Ensure scalability and continuous updates through a dynamic infrastructure.
